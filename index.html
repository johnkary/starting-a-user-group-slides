Pro Tip for boosting attendance...

Attendee Retention

* Things go great!
* People are coming!
* But same people returning...

Attendee Retention

  • Name badges
  • Someone in charge of welcoming and assimilating
  • Introduce them to 3 people:
    1. Yourself
    2. An Organizer
    3. Another Attendee
  • Ask if it's ok to engage them online
    1. Follow them on Twitter
    2. Slack channel
    3. Email list
  • Refreshments
* Badges help even me as an organizer. Especially infrequents. * Often people come and nobody talks to them... * All of this can be hard, social psychology

Attendee Retention

  • Incentive: Crypto Currency
  • Track attendance!
  • Regularly survey your attendees

Attendee Feedback

  • Contact after every meetup!
  • Negative feedback can be private / anonymous
* Meetup does this but it's impersonal.
* Specifically reach out to people who were new or haven't seen lately

Over time...

  • The same people keep coming...
  • Convert them to organizers
* Same is good! Grow to know those people more deeply. * Small group discussions are most rewarding, often after others leave.

Challenges

  • Alcohol at meetings?
  • Difficult attendees
    • Discuss in private
    • Cite code of conduct
    • Distinguish behavior from person
    • Repeat offenders will be asked to not return
    * Alcohol can be uncomfortable. * UG's are open. Someone will inevitably say something or misbehave. * Code of conduct is important.
